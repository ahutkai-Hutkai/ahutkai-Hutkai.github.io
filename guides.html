Quick Basics

How Training Works

Clicking on a training will get you stats in that training and its corresponding training. Speed will also give some power | Stamina will also give some guts | Power will also give some stamina | Guts will also give some speed and power | Wisdom will also give some speed

Getting Friendship up on support cards will allow for friendship bonus clicks on their designated card type (Speed, Stamina, Power, Guts, Wit), signified by a rainbow (often called rainbow training). The power of this is listed under a cards "Friendship bonus". This effect can be boosted by a cards Training Effectiveness, Mood Effect, and Specific Stat bonus (Like speed bonus or power bonus)

Track Types and How racing works

Umamusume all have their pwn unique apptitudes, making certain distances and running styles better or worse on them. We recommend at least having a "B" apptitude in the distance/race style in order to be effective.

The racing track Styles are Sprint/Mile/Medium/Long. With Each type having a range of distances they can be

Sprint: 1000m-1400m (roughly 300 stam required) | Mile: 1400m-1800m (roughly 500-600 stam required) | Medium: 1800m-2400m (roughly 700 - 900 stam required) | Long: 2400m-3200m (roughly 1000 - 1200 stam required)

Running races in career gives you a high amount of skill points, which allows you to buy more skills for your Uma, however you lose out on stat clicks or energy gain (races outside your career path also cost stamina)

Running Styles

Racing involves selecting your Umamusume's racing Style. Front Runner/Pace Chaser/Later Surger/End Closer.

Each Style has its own strengths and weaknesses, and choosing the right one for your Uma Musume can make a big difference in race performance.

Understanding Stats

Speed | Speed Is the most important stat in the game, as it determines the maximum top speed an Umamusume can attain. This stat indicates the potential for your Umamusume to surpass all other characters and overtake them in a race.

Stamina | Stamina determines how long an Umamusume can maintain their speed before tiring. It is crucial for endurance races and affects the character's ability to sustain high performance over extended periods.

Power | Power contributes to an Umamusume's acceleration and ability to maintain speed, especially in the middle and later stages of a race. It can help characters recover from setbacks and maintain their position in the race.

Guts | Guts is a stat that influences an Umamusume's ability to perform well under pressure, particularly in the final stretch of a race. It can help characters make a strong finish and overtake opponents in the closing moments of a race. (in the current state of the game, guts is the weakest stat)

Wisdom | Wisdom affects an Umamusume's decision-making and skill activation during races. It increases the activation chance of skills and improve the character's overall performance by allowing them to make better positioning choices during a race.
