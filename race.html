Stats Overview

All stats range from 1–2000. Values past 1200 are reduced in effectiveness. Mood affects stats by ±2% per level, and during Career mode all stats gain a temporary +400 boost.

Core Stats

Speed

Determines top speed during Late-Race and Last Spurt phases.

Stamina

Acts as HP during races. Running out drastically reduces speed.

Power

Controls acceleration, lane changing, and uphill performance.

Guts

Reduces stamina drain and boosts late-race performance.

Wit

Affects skill activation, race behavior, and consistency.

Race Phases

  • Early Race: First 1/6
  • Mid Race: 2/6–4/6
  • Late Race: 5/6
  • Last Spurt: Final 1/6

Many skills activate based on these phases.

Speed System

Target Speed vs Current Speed

Target Speed is the speed your character aims for, while Current Speed is what they are actually running. Acceleration determines how quickly Current Speed reaches Target Speed.

Average Speeds

  • Start Dash: ~17 m/s
  • Mid Race: ~20 m/s
  • Last Spurt: ~25 m/s

Acceleration

Acceleration determines how fast speed increases. It is affected by:

  • Power stat
  • Strategy
  • Race phase
  • Terrain

Acceleration is most important at the start of the Last Spurt.

Last Spurt Mechanic

At the end of the race, characters gain a speed boost if they have enough stamina.

  • High stamina = stronger spurt
  • Low stamina = delayed or weakened spurt

Positioning Mechanics

Position Keep

Controls how racers maintain their position early in the race using different modes.

Repositioning

Consumes stamina to improve position mid-race.

Securing the Lead

Uses stamina to maintain distance from opponents.

Special Mechanics

Rushing

  • Increases stamina usage
  • Changes behavior
  • Triggered randomly

Spot Struggle

Occurs between Front Runners competing for the lead.

Dueling

Triggers near the finish when racers are close together.

Stamina Contest

Boosts speed in long races if stamina is high.

Terrain & Conditions

  • Turf vs Dirt affects performance
  • Heavy terrain reduces speed
  • Bad conditions increase stamina drain

Race Measurements

Distances are measured in "horse lengths" (~2.5 meters).

  • Nose (~20 cm)
  • Head (~40 cm)
  • Neck (~80 cm)

Starting Mechanics

Each racer has a small random delay at the start.

  • Late starts reduce acceleration
  • Skills can improve or worsen starts

Key Takeaways

  • Speed matters most in the final phase
  • Acceleration is critical for winning
  • Stamina determines if you can finish strong
  • Positioning mechanics heavily affect outcomes
