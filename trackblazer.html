Trackblazer (Make a New Track)

Basic Information

Trackblazer is a scenario released in March 2026 (originally February 2022 in JP). It is the third permanent scenario after URA Finals and Unity Cup.

This scenario focuses heavily on racing and removes character-specific objectives. Instead, players must earn Grade Points to progress.

Scenario Objectives

  • Debut Race
  • 60 Grade Points
  • +300 Grade Points
  • +300 Grade Points

Some characters have reduced requirements depending on their race type.

Grade Points & Shop Coins

You earn Grade Points and Shop Coins by participating in races.

Special Shop

Shop Coins can be used to buy items that give bonuses during your run. The shop refreshes every 6 turns.

  • Stat boosts (Speed, Stamina, etc.)
  • Energy recovery items
  • Bond increases
  • Training boosts

Rivals

Some races include rivals. Beating them rewards skill hints based on race type or strategy.

Twinkle Star Climax

The final stage consists of three races. Instead of elimination, players earn Victory Points.

Unique Skill Level-Ups

Skills level up based on yearly performance and bond with the chairman.

Training & Stats

Training facilities level up through repeated use. Maximum level is 5.

  • Speed: Speed + Power
  • Stamina: Stamina + Guts
  • Power: Power + Stamina
  • Guts: Mixed stats
  • Wit: Wisdom + Energy

Conclusion

Trackblazer is a race-focused scenario that rewards strategic race selection and efficient resource use. It offers more flexibility than previous scenarios and is ideal for players who enjoy optimizing runs.
