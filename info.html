About UmaInfo

UmaInfo is a fan-made resource designed to help players understand and improve at Uma Musume: Pretty Derby. The goal of this site is to break down complex mechanics into simple, easy-to-understand guides.

Whether you're a beginner learning how stats work, or an experienced player optimizing your training runs, this site provides tools, explanations, and resources to help you succeed.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Beginner-friendly guides
  • Detailed race mechanics explanations
  • Scenario breakdowns like Trackblazer
  • External tools to improve your gameplay
